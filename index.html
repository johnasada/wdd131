Nigeria, officially known as the Federal Republic of Nigeria, is a country located in West Africa.

Nigeria is known for its rich natural resources, including oil, natural gas, and minerals. The country has a vibrant culture, with a strong tradition of music, art, and literature. Nigerian cuisine is also diverse and flavorful, with popular dishes like jollof rice, suya, and egusi soup.
